About Us

Crystal Palace Football Club, founded in 1905, is a professional football club based in Selhurst, South London, England. The team competes in the Premier League, the top tier of English football. Known for their passionate fan base and rich history, Crystal Palace has established itself as a formidable force in English football.

Our mission is to play an exciting brand of football, develop young talent, and engage with our community. We are proud of our heritage and are committed to building a bright future for the club and its supporters.

Join us at Selhurst Park to experience the thrill of live football and be part of the Crystal Palace family. Together, we soar higher!
